    Calculation Details

    Directional Accuracy is computed via walk-forward backtesting: the model trains on past-only data, predicts the next ~80 days, and scores UP/DOWN correctness — repeated 3 times across different periods. The % shown is how often it was right on data it never saw.

    🎯 ≥55% = real edge · 50–55% = marginal · <50% = worse than a coin flip on this ticker/horizon.

    Signal conviction is Weak near earnings or Fed meetings regardless of accuracy — those events introduce regime uncertainty that technical features cannot capture. P/C Ratio reflects options market positioning for the nearest expiry (display only — not in the model due to limited historical data).
