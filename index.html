    Directional Accuracy — walk-forward backtesting on data the model never saw (3 windows × ~80 days = 240 samples). ≥55% = real edge · 50–55% = marginal · <50% = worse than a coin flip for this ticker/horizon. The "Recent Predictions vs Actuals" panel above shows the last 25 of those backtested predictions with their realized outcomes — so you can verify, not just trust, the headline number.

    Conviction is based on LightGBM + Random Forest agreement only — Linear Regression is excluded from conviction scoring because it is too simple to reliably capture price dynamics. Weak conviction is forced near earnings or Fed meetings regardless of accuracy.

    Target Price is the current price ± the model's predicted % change over the selected horizon. P/C Ratio = nearest-expiry put/call volume (options market sentiment — display only, not trained into the model). VIX > 20 = elevated volatility / inflated options premiums.

    This is a directional bias filter for swing and options traders — not a real-time brokerage platform. Use the signal to establish a pre-trade directional lean, then open your broker's options chain to size and execute.
